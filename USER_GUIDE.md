## ðŸ’ª The 7-Minute Workout Routine

### Exercise List

1. **Jumping Jacks** - 30 seconds
2. **Wall Sit** - 30 seconds (chair required)
3. **Push-ups** - 30 seconds
4. **Abdominal Crunches** - 30 seconds
5. **Step-ups** - 30 seconds (chair required)
6. **Squats** - 30 seconds
7. **Triceps Dips** - 30 seconds (chair required)
8. **Plank** - 30 seconds
9. **High Knees** - 30 seconds
10. **Lunges** - 30 seconds
11. **Push-up Rotation** - 30 seconds
12. **Side Plank** - 30 seconds (each side)

### Workout Structure

- **30 seconds** of exercise
- **10 seconds** of rest
- **Total time**: ~7 minutes (plus prep time)

## ðŸ’¡ Tips & Best Practices

### Getting the Most Out of Your Workouts

1. **Consistency**: Work out at the same time each day
2. **Form First**: Focus on proper form over speed
3. **Rest Days**: Take rest days when needed
4. **Progression**: Gradually increase difficulty as you improve
5. **Hydration**: Stay hydrated before and after workouts

### Building a Habit

1. **Start Small**: Begin with the basic 7-minute routine
2. **Track Progress**: Use the app to track your consistency
3. **Celebrate Wins**: Unlock achievements and celebrate milestones
4. **Stay Motivated**: Use daily quotes and reminders
5. **Be Patient**: Building habits takes time

### Safety

- **Consult a Doctor**: If you have health concerns, consult a physician
- **Listen to Your Body**: Stop if you feel pain or discomfort
- **Warm Up**: Consider light stretching before workouts
- **Cool Down**: Stretch after completing your workout
- **Stay Hydrated**: Drink water before, during, and after

## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Do I need equipment?**  
A: You only need a chair for the wall sit, step-ups, and triceps dips exercises.

**Q: How long is the workout?**  
A: The workout is approximately 7 minutes, plus a brief prep time.

**Q: Can I do this every day?**  
A: Yes! The workout is designed to be done daily. Listen to your body and take rest days when needed.

**Q: Is this suitable for beginners?**  
A: Yes! The app includes modifications for all fitness levels, including beginners.

### Technical Questions

**Q: How do I sync with Apple Health?**  
A: Enable HealthKit in Settings and grant permissions when prompted.

**Q: Can I use the app without internet?**  
A: Yes! The app works completely offline. Only optional features like analytics may require internet.

**Q: How do I export my data?**  
A: Go to Settings > Data > Export Data and choose CSV or JSON format.

**Q: Can I reset my data?**  
A: Yes, in Settings > Danger Zone > Reset All Data. This cannot be undone.

### Apple Watch Questions

**Q: Do I need an Apple Watch?**  
A: No, the app works perfectly on iPhone and iPad without a Watch.

**Q: How do I install the Watch app?**  
A: The Watch app installs automatically when you pair your Watch and install the iPhone app.

**Q: Can I start workouts from my Watch?**  
A: Yes! You can start and complete workouts entirely from your Watch.
