## üöÄ SECTION 9: SUBMISSION PROCESS

### Step-by-Step Submission

1. **Upload Build in Xcode**
   - Open Xcode
   - Product ‚Üí Archive
   - Wait for archive to complete
   - Click "Distribute App"
   - Select "App Store Connect"
   - Follow the upload process

2. **Wait for Processing**
   - Apple processes the build (usually 10-30 minutes)
   - You'll receive an email when processing is complete
   - Check App Store Connect ‚Üí TestFlight ‚Üí Builds

3. **Complete App Store Listing**
   - Go to App Store Connect
   - Navigate to your app
   - Click "+ Version or Platform"
   - Fill in all sections above

4. **Select Build**
   - In the "Build" section, select your processed build
   - If you don't see your build, wait a few more minutes

5. **Review All Information**
   - Go through this checklist
   - Double-check all text fields
   - Preview screenshots
   - Verify URLs are working

6. **Submit for Review**
   - Click "Submit for Review"
   - Confirm submission
   - Status will change to "Waiting for Review"

7. **Wait for Review**
   - Typical review time: 24-48 hours
   - You'll receive email notifications
   - Check status in App Store Connect

## üìß SECTION 10: AFTER SUBMISSION

### Review Timeline
- **In Review:** 24-48 hours typically
- **Pending Developer Release:** Approved, waiting for you to release
- **Ready for Sale:** Live in App Store
- **Rejected:** You'll receive feedback and can fix/resubmit

### If Approved
- You can release immediately or schedule a release date
- App will appear in App Store within 24 hours of release

### If Rejected
- Read the rejection reason carefully
- Fix the issues
- Resubmit with a new build (increment build number)
- You can reply to reviewer notes in App Store Connect
