## 5. RATINGS & REVIEWS STRATEGY

### 5.1 Why Ratings Matter

- **Direct Impact:** Higher ratings = higher rankings
- **Conversion:** 4.5+ stars = 2x more downloads
- **Algorithm:** App Store favors apps with recent positive reviews

### 5.2 In-App Rating Prompt Strategy

**Best Practices:**
- Don't prompt immediately after first launch
- Prompt after positive experience (completed workout, achievement unlocked)
- Use native iOS rating prompt (not custom)
- Don't prompt too frequently (max 3x per year)

**Optimal Timing:**
1. After 3rd completed workout
2. After unlocking first achievement
3. After reaching 7-day streak
4. After positive user action (custom workout created, etc.)

**Implementation:**
- Use `SKStoreReviewController.requestReview()` (native)
- Only show on iOS 10.3+
- Respect user's "Don't Ask Again" selection

### 5.3 Review Response Strategy

**Best Practices:**
- Respond to ALL reviews (within 48 hours)
- Thank positive reviews personally
- Address negative reviews professionally
- Offer help for issues
- Show you care about users

**Response Templates:**

**Positive Review:**
```
Thank you so much! We're thrilled Ritual7 is helping you stay fit. Keep up the amazing work! üí™
```

**Negative Review (Technical Issue):**
```
Hi [Name], we're sorry to hear about this issue. We'd love to help! Please email us at [email] and we'll resolve this immediately.
```

**Negative Review (Feature Request):**
```
Thanks for the feedback! We're always improving Ritual7. This feature is on our roadmap for a future update. Stay tuned!
```

### 5.4 Review Generation Tactics

**DO:**
- ‚úÖ Provide excellent user experience
- ‚úÖ Prompt at optimal times
- ‚úÖ Make it easy to rate (native prompt)
- ‚úÖ Respond to reviews promptly

**DON'T:**
- ‚ùå Incentivize reviews (violates App Store guidelines)
- ‚ùå Fake reviews (will get you banned)
- ‚ùå Ask only happy users to rate
- ‚ùå Prompt too frequently
