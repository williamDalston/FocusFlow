## KEYWORD RESEARCH NOTES

### Best Practices:
- ✅ Use exact match phrases users search for
- ✅ Include common misspellings if space allows
- ✅ Prioritize high-volume, relevant keywords
- ✅ Test different keyword combinations monthly
- ✅ Monitor rankings daily
- ✅ Track conversion rates

### Avoid:
- ❌ Generic terms that won't help ranking ("fitness" alone)
- ❌ Keyword stuffing (use naturally)
- ❌ Duplicate keywords
- ❌ Irrelevant keywords
- ❌ Setting and forgetting (test monthly)
