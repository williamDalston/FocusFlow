# Prompt: Create Marketing and Support Pages for Pomodoro Timer App

## Context

You are tasked with creating two HTML pages for a Pomodoro Timer app called **Ritual7**:

1. **Marketing Page** (`marketing.html`) - A beautiful landing page to promote the app
2. **Support Page** (`support.html`) - A help and support page for users

The app has been refactored from a 7-minute workout app to a **Pomodoro Timer/Focus app**. All references to workouts, exercises, and fitness should be replaced with focus sessions, Pomodoro technique, and productivity concepts.

## App Information

**App Name:** Ritual7  
**App Type:** Pomodoro Timer / Focus Timer App  
**Platform:** iOS (iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch)  
**Target Audience:** Students, remote workers, professionals seeking better focus and productivity  
**Bundle ID:** com.williamalston.Ritual7

## Key Features to Highlight

### Core Features
- **Pomodoro Timer**: Classic 25-minute focus sessions with 5-minute breaks
- **Multiple Presets**: 
  - Classic Pomodoro (25 min focus / 5 min break / 15 min long break)
  - Deep Work (45 min focus / 15 min break / 30 min long break)
  - Short Sprints (15 min focus / 3 min break / 15 min long break)
  - Custom intervals support
- **Beautiful UI**: Modern SwiftUI interface with glassmorphism design
- **Sound & Haptics**: Audio feedback and vibration for phase transitions
- **Progress Tracking**: Track focus sessions, streaks, and statistics
- **Apple Watch Support**: Full Pomodoro timer companion app
- **Pomodoro Cycles**: Automatic long breaks after 4 focus sessions

### Advanced Features
- Comprehensive analytics (weekly/monthly/yearly trends)
- Achievement system with celebrations
- Smart notifications (daily reminders, streak maintenance)
- Customizable focus/break durations
- Full accessibility support (VoiceOver, Dynamic Type, high contrast)
- Multiple themes (Calm Focus, Energetic Tomato, Monochrome Pro)
- Productivity insights and recommendations
- Goal setting and tracking
